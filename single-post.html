About getting to Puebla

There are two ways to fly to Puebla form USA.

1. Fly into Mexico City international airport and take a bus to Publa.
The buses are in the airport terminals and they take you to a station in Puebla where David & I can make arrangements to pick you up or you can use UBER from your app it is available in the city as well.

How to buy your Bus ticket and Where to go in Puebla:
Getting off the plane walk over to the Bus ticket counter for directions or to buy your ticket. I suggest to buy your ticket online before you travel so you only need to find the bus locations there in the terminal. Here is the site to buy your bus tickets Bus tickets
Buy a one way ticket from Estrella Roja (company) to PUEBLA. Depending which hotel you have picked select either Plaza San Diego Cholula, or Terminal 4 Pte. Publa. (NOTE* DO NOT SELECT CAPU)
Both locations are Puebla just depends where are you staying for hotels Cholula or Puebla. If you have questions please let me know

Buses are comfortable and usually have movies and some have wifi.

2. Option two is to fly directly to Puebla, this option is more costly than flying into Mexico City but you avoid the bus route.

Puebla airport has limit flights so in US you may have to fly to a location where you can conect to the destination. David and I can help wit pick up arrangements from there to your hotel aswell.

If you will need pick up and help from us please let us know in a timely manner so we can coordinate. Thank you
